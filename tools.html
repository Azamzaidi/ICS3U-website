Cybersecurity Tools

  • Antivirus software(e.g., Avast, Norton, etc)
  • Password managers
  • Two-factor authentication (e.g., Google Authenticator)
  • Secure web browsers(keep them updated)
  • Keep your Wi‑Fi private, disable auto‑connect, and avoid public networks for sensitive logins.
  • Turn off Bluetooth/NFC when idle and never pair with unknown devices.
  • Use a VPN on public Wi‑Fi and avoid logging into banking or email there.
  • Limit app permissions, uninstall apps you don’t use, and avoid unknown APKs.
  • Enable device lock, auto‑lock quickly, and hide notifications on the lock screen.
  • Share minimal personal info online and review privacy settings on social media regularly.
