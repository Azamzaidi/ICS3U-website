Cybersecurity Tips

Visit the offcial Canadian government site for more guidance Get Cyber Safe

  • Use strong unique passwords for all accounts.
  • Never click on suspicious links.
  • Do not reuse password for different accounts
  • Look for secure websites (https:// and padlock symbols).
  • Turn on multi‑factor authentication so no one can access your accounts with just a password.
  • Review your privacy settings often to limit how much personal info apps and websites can see.
  • Keep your browser clean by removing old extensions you don’t use anymore.
  • Check your account activity pages regularly to spot any unusual logins early.
  • Use device encryption so your data stays protected even if your phone or laptop is lost.
